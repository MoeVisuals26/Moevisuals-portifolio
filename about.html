The Filmmaker

Mohamed Wael

I'm a Cairo-based videographer and visual storyteller.

"Great video isn't just recorded — it's crafted."

Four years ago, I was working as a salesman — a world away from filmmaking. A growing passion for visual storytelling pulled me toward a new path, and I made the leap into shooting brand campaigns and building a career as a videographer. Since then, I've never stopped evolving — constantly adopting the latest tools and technologies, from cinema-grade cameras to AI-powered production, to keep every project at the cutting edge.

I work across every field because great stories exist everywhere — in a product, in a song, in a ceremony, in a person.
